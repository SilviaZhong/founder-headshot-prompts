# Founder Headshot Skill

## Goal
Turn an original ID photo into a realistic, professionally photographed founder-style portrait that looks suitable for:
- startup competitions
- pitch decks
- demo day materials
- AI business presentations
- company team pages
- personal About pages

The output must look like a real photograph, not an obvious AI-generated image.

## Required input
The user should provide:
- an original ID photo or passport-style portrait
- optional preferences for age impression, hairstyle, skin tone, outfit, glasses, and overall vibe

## Default visual style
- realistic
- photorealistic
- startup founder portrait
- young tech entrepreneur
- clean and minimal
- professional but not stiff
- modern but understated
- not overly formal
- not overly retouched
- not obviously AI-generated

## Core rules
1. Preserve the real face, proportions, and identity from the input photo.
2. Do not over-beautify.
3. Avoid corporate stock-photo styling.
4. Avoid overly polished skin, fake lighting, and “AI face”.
5. Prefer soft studio lighting and a light neutral background.
6. Prefer relaxed seated or lightly angled poses over stiff straight-on passport posture.
7. Use minimal founder-style clothing:
   - navy / deep indigo / dark gray outer layer
   - dark brown / charcoal / black inner top
   - natural wrinkles allowed
8. Keep the final result suitable for decks, team pages, and business use.

## Output format
When generating or refining a prompt, structure it with:
- purpose
- identity preservation
- composition
- expression
- lighting and background
- outfit
- realism constraints
- negative constraints

## Female defaults
- youthful but real
- natural skin texture
- minimal styling
- founder / product-builder vibe
- no excessive sweetness or influencer glam

## Male defaults
- youthful but real
- relaxed professional
- natural skin texture
- founder / product-builder vibe
- no stiff corporate look

## Negative constraints
Avoid:
- over-whitened skin
- plastic skin
- stock-photo business look
- suit-and-tie corporate portrait unless explicitly requested
- exaggerated retouching
- AI beauty filter look
- heavy makeup or flashy accessories
- cluttered office backgrounds
- fake cinematic effects

## Final check
Before finishing, verify:
- Does it still look like the real person?
- Does it look like a real photographed portrait?
- Is it suitable for startup / AI / pitch-deck use?
- Is the styling minimal and credible?
